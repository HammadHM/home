Welcome to my homepage to find out all about the Central Processing Unit!

Also known as central processor, and main processor

The Central Processing Unit is an essential component of the computer that processes instructions, performs calculations, and executes programs. It runs the computer applications and the system's OS! The CPU continually accepts input from the user or software running on the system. It processes the information and produces output, which may be stored by an app or displayed on the screen.
