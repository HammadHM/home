Understanding CPU specs

Cores

The CPU is made with micro-processors, also known as CPU cores, these microprocessors
are what do the calculations; meaning that more cores are what significantly boost your
computer's performance. The typical amount of cores are 4 cores.

Clock Speed

The CPU has to do a set number of instructions quickly to make the computer run
smoothly, the speed of the CPUs work cycle can be told by the Clock speed - usually
Ghz. Its not as important as core count, however it does matter. The typical speed
is 3.5Ghz-4.2Ghz.

Cache

CPU cache is very fast on-board memory, much faster then RAM.
With more L3 Cache, your most often used programs are loaded much quicker.

Future Trends

The future trends of the CPUs are quite straightforward. More cores,
higher clock speed, and more cache. However, CPU's price trend seems to be going
up. The materials, intensive research and mechanical machinary are going to make the price
go higher in the future.
